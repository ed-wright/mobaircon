About Us
  • We are a fully mobile service offering car air conditioning re-gas service for all cars. We are able to visit you anywhere including your work or home.
  • Fully licenced to work on vehicle air conditioning as required by UK law.
  • Re-gas service available 7 days a week including Sundays.
  • Used and trusted by local garages and car sales forecourts.
  • Discounts for working on more than one vehicle during the same appointment.

  • Using air fresheners to stop the smell from your AC?

    We offer professional treatments applied directly to your vents to kill mould and bacteria.

Our Services Include:
  • Temperature Check
  • Recover any existing refrigerant from the System
  • Remove old existing oil from the System
  • Vacuum the Air Conditioning System to remove any moisture
  • Re Gas Air Conditioning System with R134a refrigerant to vehicle specific levels including a UV Dye
  • Replace PAG oils as required
  • Leak test
